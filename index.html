The Verdict Is Yours

01

Swipe to Judge

Cases arrive on your desk daily. A simple swipe left or right — but nothing about this job is simple. Your choice echoes through the city.

02

Four Forces Watch

The People, the Authorities, the Press, and the Law itself all track your every ruling. Satisfy one — and risk offending another.

03

Hundreds of Cases

Corruption, family drama, political crimes, moral grey zones. No two playthroughs are the same. The city has no shortage of conflict.

04

Stories Unfold

Characters return. Decisions cascade. An arc begun in week one may explode in week seven. Your past verdicts define your future.
