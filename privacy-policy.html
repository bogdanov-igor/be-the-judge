Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how Be The Judge ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the app:

  • Usage data — anonymous gameplay statistics (Firebase Analytics): session length, feature usage, crash reports.
  • Purchase data — records of in-app purchases processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store payment details.
  • Advertising data — if you interact with ads, Google AdMob may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Device identifiers — anonymous identifiers used for analytics and ad serving.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the gameplay experience
  • To process and verify in-app purchases
  • To show relevant advertisements
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — analytics and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — advertising platform. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing for purchases.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data may be shared only with the third-party services listed above, to the extent necessary to operate the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Be The Judge is rated 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months as per Firebase's default settings. Purchase records are retained as required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
